Race Information

Whether you’re a joy runner or a seasoned athlete, Koh Dach Marathon offers the perfect island route. Explore our race options below and pick your challenge!

5km Joy Run

Start Time: 7:00 AM

Distance: 5 km

Difficulty: Easy

Fee: $8

Route: Smooth trails alongside silk farms.

10km Island Trail

Start Time: 6:40 AM

Distance: 10 km

Difficulty: Moderate

Fee: $10

Route: Mix of dirt paths and paved roads with beautiful river views.

Additional Information

  • Start/Finish Line: Amra (អមរា)
  • Checkpoints: Every 2.5 km (water & medical aid)
  • Terrain: Cement road & Paved village roads
  • Hydration Points: At every checkpoints
  • Safety Guidelines: Follow staff directions and stay hydrated
  • Ferry Info: Take the ferry from Chroy Changvar port for free (operates from 5:30 AM)
