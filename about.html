About Koh Dach Run

Koh Dach, also known as Silk Island, is a peaceful island on the Mekong River just a short ferry ride from Phnom Penh. Our marathon celebrates the island‚Äôs natural beauty and rich silk-weaving culture while supporting local communities.

Our Mission

The Koh Dach Run aims to promote health, eco-tourism, and cultural preservation. Proceeds will support local silk-making families and rural schools on the island.
