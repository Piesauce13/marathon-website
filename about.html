Our Story: Running for Silk Island

We are more than just a marathon; we are a community-driven initiative dedicated to celebrating and supporting the vibrant culture of Koh Dach.

Our Mission & Motivation

The Koh Dach Run began with a simple idea: to bring people together through movement, nature, and community. By blending health, eco-tourism, and cultural preservation, we aim to create an event that benefits both visitors and local communities alike.

Our key objectives are to:

  • Inspire healthy lifestyles and encourage community engagement.
  • Support local silk weaving families and rural schools on the island.
  • Boost eco-tourism and celebrate the unique culture of Koh Dach.
