Event Location

The race will be held on the beautiful island of Koh Dach, just a short ferry ride from Phnom Penh!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to Koh Dach?

You can reach Koh Dach via the ferry terminal located on Highway 6. The ferry ride is short and scenic, and there will be clear signs directing participants on race day.

Where does the race start?

The starting line for all races will be near the main pagoda on the island. We will provide a detailed map and instructions in your race packet upon registration.

What happens if it rains?

The Koh Dach Run is a rain-or-shine event. In the case of severe weather that poses a danger to participants, we will notify everyone of any delays or cancellations via email and our social media channels.
