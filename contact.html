FAQ

How do I get to Koh Dach?

Take the ferry from Chroy Changvar ferry port. The ride takes around 10 minutes.

Where does the race start?

At the main ferry pier on Koh Dach island.

What happens if it rains?

The run will continue unless severe weather occurs.
